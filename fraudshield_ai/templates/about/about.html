About FraudShield AI

AI-powered financial protection for the modern world

🛡️ Our Mission

FraudShield AI was built to give individuals and businesses enterprise-grade fraud detection without the enterprise price tag. Powered by machine learning models trained on real fraud patterns, we help you identify suspicious transactions before they cause damage.

🤖 Technology Stack

Backend

ML Models
  • 🌲 Random Forest Classifier
  • 🔬 Isolation Forest (Anomaly)
  • 📏 Standard Scaler

⚠️ Disclaimer
